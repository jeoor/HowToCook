# How to Make Lemonade

![Finished Lemonade](./柠檬水.jpg)

Estimated Cooking Difficulty: ★

## Essential Ingredients and Tools

- Ingredients
  - Lemon
  - Fruit Honey
  - Ice (optional)
- Tools
  - Shaker

## Measurements

One serving, approximately 500 ml

- Lemon: 40–45 g
- Fruit Honey: 40–45 g
- Ice: A few cubes (optional)

## Instructions

- Weigh 40–45 g of lemon and place it in the shaker
- Cover the shaker and shake vigorously about 10 times
- Add 40–45 g of fruit honey
- Add water
- Shake well to mix
- Finally, add ice to taste
